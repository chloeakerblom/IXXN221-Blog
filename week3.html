Week Three - Weekend Progress (31/07/22)

Over the weekend I have been working on creating the user persona and empathy map for my chosen fictional character. I have been having a bit of trouble deciding who to use but, after creating a long list of possible characters, I have settled on using Homer Simpson. he has a lot of personality traits and weaknesses that I can develop a website for. I have decided to focus on his lack of DIY skill for the topic of my website. In several episodes he attempts to build or fix things that go wrong, in particular when he attempts to build a BBQ but drops the instructions in the cement. I am thinking of creating a website that will help people with basic DIY skills, bith around simple projects and tool how-tos.
