Divs and Flexbox

The video lecture this week was about using divs and flexbox. Divs are something that I had used before, however the flexbox material was new to me. Whenever I have had to code a website before I always had difficulty trying to centre items and make responsive columns and rows that stayed in the correct proportions. This method of doing it (using 'display: flex') is a really easy and clear way of getting it to work and is definitely something I will use going forward. It will make laying out items within divs a lot easier and will make it more efficient when it comes to making the website responsive.
